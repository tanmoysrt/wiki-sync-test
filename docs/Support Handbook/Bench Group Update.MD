### How to deploy new release on a bench group.

* Open the bench group dashboard you wish to update or you can click on **Release Group** on site doc and then click **Visit Dashboard**.
* On Bench Group Dashboard, click on show update (if the bench group has update to deploy) and click **Deploy**.

![deploy bench group](/private/files/Screenshot%202023-02-23%20at%206.24.18%20PM.png)  



> If you see a blank page on pressing Deploy, switch to the team of the bench group. You can impersonate team from the bench group dashboard.
