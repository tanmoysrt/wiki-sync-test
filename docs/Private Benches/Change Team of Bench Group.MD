You can change the team of the site by using the **Transfer Bench Group** feature.

1.  Select Transfer Bench Group option from the Actions tab in your Bench Group's dashboard.

![](/private/files/Screenshot%202025-06-18%20at%2012.49.22%E2%80%AFPM.png)

2. Fill in the fields and click on Submit

![](/private/files/Screenshot%202025-06-18%20at%2012.50.31%E2%80%AFPM.png)

3. The team that is supposed to receive the ownership will now receive a mail to accept the ownership. Click on **Accept Bench Group Transfer** to accept the ownership of the Bench Group.


![](/private/files/6592bd82-8328-4ff5-b98a-7dad41081fdb.jpeg)
