#### **Creating a dedicated server:-**

From the [Servers](https://frappecloud.com/dashboard/servers) tab on the dashboard, click on **New** **Server**![](../files/j3Zxfup.png)  


Click on the **dedicated server** button.![](../files/Erru0pr.png)  


Enter the name of your new server you want and select the region for the same.![](../files/SrFJ9TT.png)  


Select the plan type **standard server** or **premium server** hosting.![](../files/Y0IABgo.png)  


Next, select the plan for the **Application** **server** and **database** **server**.![](../files/45J111V.png)![](../files/fXo8hRQ.png)  


Once you have selected the plans, you will see a summary at the bottom you can confirm and click on **Create Server** to continue.![](../files/rwoYE65.png)
